HIT ME UP!

December, 2016

Hit Me Up! (HMU) is a hangouts application made by and for Columbia students. Looking for a friend to grab a coffee with before class? Post on HMU and see if anyone else is free. Use HMU to meet students on campus with interests that align with your own, or simply to coordinate plans with your friends in one centralized application.

Collaborators: Jason Lei, Emily Song, Monica Ting, Jiaying Tang
Developed using Flask and MySQL.
