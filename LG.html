LUNAR GALA: FLUX

February, 2016

Lunar Gala is an annual student-run cultural and performance showcase at Columbia University. This year's sold-out event filled some 600 seats and was put together with participation from all across the university community, including a variety of campus partners, corporate sponsors, and over 150 student participants. YouTube singer-songwriter Jason Chen also came from LA to perform and raise awareness for this year's charity, Heart2Heart, a Shanghai-based nonprofit dedicated to underprivileged children undergoing heart surgery.
